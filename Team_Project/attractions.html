Attractions

We offer a variety of exciting and active attractions for everyone, from the SportZone, WaterPark, a Master Chef’s kitchen toa picnic zone.

Attractions for Kids

  • The Kids’ SportZone
  • Bunny Water Park
  • The Magic
The Kids’ SportZone

Let your little ones be active with our SportZone. It offers a variety of field games, including basketball, soccer, American football.
Bunny Water Park

Relax and have fun in our newly built water park. There is a water playground with slides, a wading pool, a shaded section, and many more.
The Magic

Let your kids create and thrive. The room offers plenty of fun art activities to boost kids’ creativity. Parents are welcome to join as well.

Attractions for Family

  • Alica’s Kitchen
  • Bunny Resort Trail
  • Bunny Garden
Alica’s Kitchen

Learn how to cook with our Chef Alica in a very modern, well-equipped, and kid-friendly kitchen.
Bunny Resort Trail

Spend some time in nature by exploring the amazing Mont-Tremblant National Park on foot or by bicycles or even skiting with equipment provided free at the resort.
Bunny Garden

Have a wonderful time with your family in our beautiful garden. We have plenty picnic areas. Fire pits and barbecue stations are available on demand.
